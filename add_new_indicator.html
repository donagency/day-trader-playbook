What is the Cumulative TICK Indicator?

The Cumulative TICK indicator is a powerful market sentiment gauge that tracks the real-time cumulative sum of NYSE TICK readings throughout the trading session. Unlike the standard TICK indicator that shows moment-to-moment readings, the cumulative version provides a running total that reveals the underlying market bias and momentum.

💡 Key Insight

When the cumulative TICK reaches extreme readings above +1,500 or below -1,500, it often signals strong directional momentum that can be used to confirm NQ breakout setups.
